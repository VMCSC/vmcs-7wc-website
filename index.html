7 Week Challenge

April 24th - June 13th, 2021

Learn something new.

Create something incredible.

Week 1

Learnathon

Admission to MasseyHacks VII is completely free! We want to give the largest possible amount of people the opportunity to participate.

Week 2

Algorithms

Our event will be held through Discord. Workshops and ceremonies will be delivered through a web-conferencing solution.

Week 3/4

Game Jam

Any student in grade 7 to grade 12 can register to be a hacker. If you've graduated from high school, we welcome you to apply as a mentor!

Week 5

Hardware

Not at all — in fact, we encourage newcomers to come and participate in MasseyHacks. We’ll teach you the fundamentals of programming, tech essentials, and loads more!

Week 6

Web Development

You can go solo and create something by yourself, but we encourage you to form a team beforehand — everything's better with friends. Teams can have up to 4 members. Don't worry if you don't have a team at the event, as we will have a team formation event for hackers to find potential team members.

Week 7

Hackenger Hunt

To ensure an event that is safe and enjoyable for everyone, we ask that hackers follow the MLH code of conduct. If you have any questions or would like to inform us of any issues, please email us at hello@masseyhacks.ca.

Finale

MasseyHacks

To ensure an event that is safe and enjoyable for everyone, we ask that hackers follow the MLH code of conduct. If you have any questions or would like to inform us of any issues, please email us at hello@masseyhacks.ca.
